About Bridge Online School

Connecting passionate learners with world-class bridge instructors

Our Mission

At Bridge Online School, we believe that the art of bridge should be accessible to everyone, regardless of location or experience level. Our mission is to connect passionate learners with world-class bridge instructors through innovative online learning experiences.

We strive to preserve and promote the intellectual beauty of bridge while making it relevant and exciting for new generations of players.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Bridge Online School emerged from a simple observation: while bridge remains one of the most intellectually stimulating card games ever created, learning opportunities were often limited by geography and traditional teaching methods.

Our founders, themselves passionate bridge players and educators, envisioned a platform where the best instructors could reach students worldwide, breaking down barriers and fostering a global community of bridge enthusiasts.

Today, we're proud to host teachers who have competed at the highest levels, including world champions and international masters.
